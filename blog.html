The blog

Here, you can find a wide range of projects that are listed. Each takes a good amount of time, energy and recourses to complete; therefore, please excuse the irregular updates.

Each blog goes through the creation cycle, this entails a stage for the idea to transition from a thought to something feasible. Afterwards, the idea is planned in great detail, making sure that the right equipment is available and that the project is achievable. Once all the recourses are together, then the actual creation stage can commence. Here, anything and everything will go wrong. Finally, once everything is completed, is it finally time to write a full blog post about it to share with the audience.
